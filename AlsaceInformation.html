Information about Alsace

General Information

In 2006, vines were grown on over 15,000 hectares (over 37,000 acres). 78% of the wine was classified as Alsace wines, 18% Crémant d'Alsace, and 4% Alsace Grand Cru.

Wine Properties

The Alsace region in France produces primarily white wines. Along with Austia and Germany, it produces some of the most noted dry Rieslings in the world.


Alsace wines are aromatic, floral and spicy.
