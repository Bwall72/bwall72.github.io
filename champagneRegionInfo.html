Information about Champagne

General Information

The Champagne Region is the most northern of France’s major vineyards, centered on the towns of Rheims and Epernay.

Wine Properties

Champagne is by EU law, a special designation to wine produced exclusively in Champagne, France. Champagne is famous for its all-important bubbles and historic prestige. The wine also has a history of a dedicated consumer base.

Notable Wine Producers

The most popular producers incldue Krug;, Mumm, Bollinger and Heidsieck, Moët & Chandon, and Taittinger.
