Information about Region

General Information

Bordeaux contains a host of large and overlapping wine regions, centered on the city of Bordeaux itself. There are nearly 50 appellations d'origine contrôlée (AOCs) in Bordeaux alone.

Wine Properties

Red and white wines are invariably blended in Bordeaux. The following grape varietals are permissibly grown: Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Cabernet Franc, Malbec and Petit Verdot.
